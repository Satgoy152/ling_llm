### Problem 3 (20 points)

(a) Translate into English:

14. Navila vivila biyamata tomwaya mtona? - How many women looked after this child?
15. Bikamkwamsi kweyu vivila minasina. - The women will eat four pigs.
16. Amagudina gwadi lekota? - Which child arrived?
17. Tevila tauwau bigisesi gugwadi gudigasisi? - How many men saw that child?
18. Legisesi ketala waga vivila minasiwena. - The clever women will see the canoes.

One of these sentences has two possible translations. Give them both:
- Navila vivila biyamata tomwaya mtona? - How many women looked after this child? / How many women will look after this child?

(b) Translate into Kilivila:

19. How many canoes did those old women see? - Navila waga leyamatasi minasiwena?

21. How many children will eat these pigs? - Navila gwadi magudiwena kwevila lekamkwamsi minasina?
