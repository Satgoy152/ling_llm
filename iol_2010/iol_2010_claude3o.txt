The key patterns are:
- Prohibitive mood ends in -ar, -u, or -i
- Future class I ends in -ara, -ura, or -una 
- Future classes II and III are the same, ending in -a or -ula
- The stem shows predictable consonant and vowel alternations between the prohibitive and future forms
