**Problem 3**

(a) The correct correspondences are:

Drehu - English
- drai-hmitrötr - calendar
- gaa-hmitrötr - church
- i-drai - bone
- i-jun - coast
- i-wahnawa - wall
- jun - awl
- ngöne-gejë - bunch of bananas
- ngöne-uma - skeleton
- nyine-thin - sanctuary
- uma-hmitrötr - Sunday

Cemuhî - English
- a-pulut - bed
- ba-bwén - animal
- ba-jié - fork
- bé-ôdu - cup
- bé-tii - pencil
- bé-wöli - coast
- bé-wöli-wöta - to write
- tii - twilight
- wöta - spur

(b) The words wahnawa and drai in Drehu mean 'wall' and 'bone' respectively. The words wöli and pulut in Cemuhî mean 'coast' and 'bed' respectively.

(c) In Drehu, i-bii translates to 'your bee' and tusi-hmitrötr translates to 'Sunday book'.

**Problem 5**
