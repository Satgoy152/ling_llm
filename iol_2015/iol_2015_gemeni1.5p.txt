## Problem 3 (Soundex)

**(a) Soundex Code Generation:**

1. **Retain the first letter** of the surname.
2. **Remove the following letters**: 'h', 'w', and vowels ('a', 'e', 'i', 'o', 'u').
3. **Assign numbers to remaining consonants** based on this table:
    * 1: b, f, p, v
    * 2: c, g, j, k, q, s, x, z
    * 3: d, t
    * 4: l
    * 5: m, n
    * 6: r
4. **Replace consecutive duplicate numbers** with a single instance.
5. **Remove any remaining vowels**.
6. **Truncate or pad with zeros** to obtain a four-character code (one letter followed by three numbers).

## Problem 5 (Somali Poetry)

**(a) Structure of Masafo:**

A masafo half-line consists of:

1. **Seven syllables.**
2. **Two or three words**, never one or more than three.
3. **The penultimate syllable** always bears the main stress of the line.
