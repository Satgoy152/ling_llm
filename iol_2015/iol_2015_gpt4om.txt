### Problem 3

**(a) Describe How a Soundex Code is Produced:**

1. **Retain the first letter** of the surname.
2. **Replace each consonant** (except the first letter) with its corresponding Soundex digit:
   - B, F, P, V → 1
   - C, G, J, K, Q, S, X, Z → 2
   - D, T → 3
   - L → 4
   - M, N → 5
   - R → 6
3. **Ignore consecutive duplicates** of the same digit.
4. **Truncate or pad** the result to ensure it is exactly 4 characters long.
