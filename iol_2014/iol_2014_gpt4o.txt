### Problem 4 (20 points)

**(a) Translate into English:**

8. edèì ânò nwa. sese ozyi à?
   - Translation: "Will this man frighten the thief?"

   amemùrè wei ga ò. nwa. sese ozyi.
   - Translation: "The girl said that he[the man] will frighten the thief."

   - Translation: "Did this girl not kill the youth?"

   amemùré le. lemù wei ga òki wúesè a.modhyòmù â. nò.
   - Translation: "The girl said that he[the youth] did not kill the youth."
