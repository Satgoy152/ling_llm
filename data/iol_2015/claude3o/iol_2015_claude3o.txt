Problem 3:

(a) Soundex code steps:
1. Retain the first letter of the surname 
2. Drop all vowels and Y, W, H 
3. Replace consonants with digits:
   B, F, P, V → 1
   C, G, J, K, Q, S, X, Z → 2
   D, T → 3
   L → 4
   M, N → 5
   R → 6
4. If two adjacent letters encode to the same digit, keep only one 
5. Keep the first three digits after the letter, padding with 0s if less than three
