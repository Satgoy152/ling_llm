### Problem 4: Syntax and Tone Changes
#### Answers:
- **(a)**
  1. `Did this man frighten the thief?`  
  The girl said that he (this man) did frighten the thief.
  2. `Will the deceived girl kill this youth?`  
  The deceived girl said that she (the deceived girl) would kill this youth.
  3. `This thief said that the child did not heal the man.`  
  or  
  `This thief said that the child would not heal the man.`

- **(b)**
  1. `okàa kí.nonò a.modhyòmú koʔlomù ânò à?`  
  `a.mò wei ga ó. ki.nono a.modhyòmú koʔlomù ânò.`
  2. `a. vùràmú nu. amù â. nò nwa. sese edèì à?`  
  `a. vùràmú nu. amù â. nò wei ga òkí nwa. sese edèì.`

- **(c)**
  - Nouns are not affected by any grammatical tone changes when placed at the very end of the sentence, before `à` or before `ânò`.  
  - Base form for 'thief': `ozyi`  
  - Base form for 'girl': `amemùrè`

### Problem 5: Spatial Relations and Body Parts
#### Answers:
- **(a)**
  - `Páá` — to put
  - `Páá náng nú kò` — to be dying (to put + foot + edge + hole)
  - `Páá sèè` — to envy (to put + liver)
  - `búmá ýık` — eyelash (hair + eyes)
  - `búmá zù ýık` — eyebrow (hair + above, on + eyes)
  - `dáng gòk` — poisonous snake (bad + snake)
  - `d́ı fò` — good field (good + field)
  - `d́ı sèè` — happiness (good + liver)
  - `kò ýık` — eye socket (hole + eyes)
  - `kò zòk` — nostril (hole + nose)
  - `náng ẃı` — foot (foot + human)
  - `nú fò` — field edge (edge + field)
  - `nú lébé` — tip of the tongue (edge + tongue)
  - `sèè ẃı` — liver (liver + human)
  - `ýık` — at the surface (face)
  - `ýık ẃı` — face/eyes (face/eyes + human)
  - `zù` — above, on (head)

- **(b)**
  - `búmá zù` — hair (on the head) (hair + head)
  - `kò` — hole
  - `lébé gòk` — snake's tongue (tongue + snake)
  - `lébé ẃı` — tongue (tongue + human)

- **(c)**
  - `sèè` — at the center (liver)
  - `zù ẃı` — head (head + human)
  - `dáng sèè` — displeasure (bad + liver)
  - `zòk ẃı` — nose (nose + human)
