### Problem 2: Writing Systems
#### Answers:
- **(a)**
  1. `runtiyas` — deer
  2. `patis` — foot
  3. `harnisas` — fortress
  4. `iziyanta` — they made
  5. `turpis` — bread
  6. `tarhunzas` — thunder
  7. `hawis` — sheep
  8. `sanawas` — good
  9. `nimuwizas` — son
  10. `zitis` — man
  11. `piyanti` — they give
  12. `hantawatis` — king
  13. `istaris` — hand

- **(b)**
  1. `tyis` — thunder (tarhunzas)
  2. `f4_` — son (nimuwizas)

- **(c)**
  1. `´` — ta
  2. `%` — king (hantawatis)
  3. `W` — wa, wi
  4. `t` — ta
  5. `s` — s(a)
  6. `T` — ti, foot (patis)
  7. `5` — zi, man (zitis)

### Problem 3: Syntax and Grammar
#### Answers:
- **(a)**
  1. `magasi argi ajomirra.` — The thieves are striking us.
  2. `ay kanarriːg baːbki alletirsi.` — I repaired the door for the neighbour.
  3. `hanu tirtiːg elirsu.` — The donkey found the owners.
  4. `tirti argi kamgi deːccirsa.` — The owners gave us the camel.
  5. `ay darbadki bijaːndi.` — I will buy the chicken.

- **(b)**
  1. `kanarriːcci tirtki beyyeːg atirra.` — The neighbours are giving the necklace to the owner.
  2. `jahal argi walgi jaːndeːccirsu.` — The young man bought the dog for us.
  3. `ar mangi bimaːgru.` — We will steal him.
  4. `tirti magasiːg jomirsa.` — The owners struck the thieves.
  5. `wali sarkaːygi darbadiːg eltirsa.` — The dogs found the chickens for the coward.

### Problem 4: Word Formation and Meaning
#### Answers:
- **(a)**
  1. `gu-na vaala` — water-possessor canoe (canoe)
  2. `ka’ik` — picture/shadow
  3. `ka’ik-gu` — picture+water (mirror)
  4. `kla-wun` — to get-1st person sg (I got)
  5. `laavu` — banana
  6. `laavu-ga` — banana+leaves (book)
  7. `ni’bu` — ground, land
  8. `ni’bu-na vaala` — ground, land-possessor canoe (car)
  9. `nya-ka’ik` — sun+picture (watch)
  10. `vi` — spear
  11. `vi’-wun` — to see-1st person sg (I saw)
  12. `walini’ba-na bâk` — white people-possessor pig (cow)
  13. `walini’ba-na gu` — white people-possessor water (carbonated beverage, alcohol)
  14. `walini’ba-na vi` — white people-possessor spear (rifle)

- **(b)**
  1. `vaala` — canoe
  2. `gu` — water
  3. `vi’` — to see

- **(c)**
  1. `pig` — bâk
  2. `banana leaves` ≡ `book` — laavuga (due to similarity of shape and material)
  3. `sun` — nya
  4. `white people` — walini’ba

### Problem 5: Morphological Rules
#### Answers:
- **(a)**
  1. `utnhantxi` — is it in my house?
