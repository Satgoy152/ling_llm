### Problem 1

(a) Write in digits:

1. **moeto waroewo**
   - "moeto" suggests that the numeral is in the hundreds, likely involving multiples of 100.
   - "waroewo" means 38, so "moeto waroewo" would be \(100 + 38 = 138\).

2. **ijema joka rati**
   - "ijema" is related to 39.
   - "joka rati" means 38.
   - Therefore, "ijema joka rati" would be \(39 + 38 = 77\).

3. **moeto wido ma benomi ka wi**
   - "moeto" indicates hundreds, but "wido" and "benomi" are not explicitly clear from the given numerals. 
   - Breaking down each part:
     - "moeto wido" might mean 100 + some value.
     - "benomi" is 16 and "wi" could imply addition.
     - From the structure and given numerals, "moeto wido ma benomi ka wi" = 129. 

   So the digit representation:
   - moeto waroewo: **138**
   - ijema joka rati: **77**
   - moeto wido ma benomi ka wi: **129**

(b) Write out in Ekari:

1. **1**: wija

2. **19**: benomima rati ka wija (16 + 2 + 1 = 19)

3. **26**: moeto wija ma ije (20 + 6 = 26)

4. **104**: moeto idibima rati (100 + 4 = 104)

5. **292**: moeto idibima rati ma moeto wija ma benomi (100 + 100 + 100 - 16 + 8 = 292)

   Two ways for 26:
   - moeto wija ma ije
   - benomima rati ka wija
