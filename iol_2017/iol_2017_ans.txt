### Problem 3: Linguistic Rules and Sentence Structures
#### Answers:
- **(a)**
  1. `múlóʒí mwámónà ʒìtéténbwá` — Did the sorcerer see the stars?
  2. `ʒìtéténbwá ʒjálà mùdjúlù` — There are stars in the sky.
  3. `ŋgákínà` — I danced.
  4. `djálá djámónà màhónʒò mùlwándá` — Did the man see the bananas in Luanda?

- **(b)**
  1. `ŋgáʃíká` — Did I sing?
  2. `mútú mwásáŋgá múlóʒì nì dìkámbà mùkìtándà` — The person met the sorcerer and the friend in the square.
  3. `djálá djámí djámónà dìkúŋgù` — My man (husband) saw the cave.
  4. `mùlwándà mwálà àlóʒì; àlóʒí álà mùlwándà` — There are sorcerers in Luanda.
