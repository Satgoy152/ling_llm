**Problem 2: Ancient Greek**

**a. Matching:**

| Greek Phrase | English Translation |
|--------------|---------------------|
| A. ho tōn hyiōn dulos | 1. the slave of the sons |
| B. hoi tōn dulōn cyrioi | 2. the masters of the slaves |

| G. ho tōn adelphōn oicos | 7. the house of the brothers |

**Compound Words & Expressions:**

* suno jelo: well-intentioned, literally "good yellow"
* tomo tawa telo: restaurant, literally "house four water" (referring to a table for four)
* ilo suno: prophet, literally "tool good"
* telo jelo: lantern, literally "water yellow"
* jan ilo: robot, literally "person tool"
* jan toki: orthodoxy, literally "person language"
* supa lape: bed, literally "table sleep"
* supa moku: dinner table, literally "table food"
* ma tomo: city, literally "land house"
* wile moku: hungry, literally "want food"
* tawa linja: boat, literally "four line"
* wile pona: movement, literally "want good"
* telo kiwen: ice, literally "water stone"
* lipu toki: book, literally "flat thing language"
* wile lawa: dominant, literally "want head"
* linja lawa: thorn, literally "line head"
* tomo moku: piss, literally "house food" (euphemism)
* linja kiwen: hair, literally "line stone"

**b. Translations of Simple Words:**

* toki: language, to speak, word
* pona: good, simple, to fix, correct, repair

**c. Literal Translation of the Language Name:** 
**Toki Pona** literally translates to **"good language"** or **"simple language."**
