#### (a) Explanation of the Error
The animal species that is regarded as “old women” in one of the myths of the Dyirbal people is the **dragonfly**. The linguist considered there was an error because the sentence involving the dragonfly used the human gender marker "bala," which is typically used for animate beings, such as humans or animals with a special cultural significance. In the Dyirbal myth, dragonflies are considered to have a special cultural significance, thus justifying the use of the human gender marker.
