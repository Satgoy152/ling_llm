#### Assignment 2: Explain when the marks ` and ´ are used in Catalan

In Catalan, the ` (grave accent) and ´ (acute accent) marks indicate the stressed syllable in a word, and they can also denote vowel quality. Generally:
- ` is used on the vowels a, e, and o to indicate an open vowel sound.
- ´ is used on the vowels e and i to indicate a closed vowel sound.

#### Assignment 3: Determine which syllable is stressed in words without accent marks

In Catalan, if a word has no accent marks, the stress typically falls on the penultimate syllable if the word ends in a vowel, an -s, or an -n. If the word ends in any other consonant, the stress falls on the last syllable.
